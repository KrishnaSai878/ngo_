About NGO Connect

Connecting NGOs, volunteers, and donors to maximize social impact.

Our Mission

NGO Connect is a national platform that brings together organizations, volunteers, and donors. We help NGOs showcase opportunities, volunteers find and book 2-hour slots, and donors support causes they care about. The platform offers skills-based matching, messaging, resources, projects, leaderboards, analytics, and more.

For Volunteers

Discover relevant opportunities, book convenient time slots, and track your impact.

For NGOs

Create events with time slots, manage bookings, share resources, and track projects.

For Donors

Find verified NGOs, follow progress, and support initiatives that align with your values.
